The Computer Science and Engineering department of JNTUK UCEN proudly celebrates its Fourth Technical Symphosium SpeCSEr-2K22 on 25th March 2023 and cordially invites all the students across the nation for active participation.

The objective of SpeCSEr is to create a suitable ambience for facilitating and nurturing the technical interactions so as to widen the knowledge of the students and create a platform to demonstrate the talents with in the students and thus create medium to share and to gain knowledge.
